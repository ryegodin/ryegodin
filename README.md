## About Me

> *"Sitting on a chair."*

I'm **Ryan Godin**, a researcher of **quantum computing**, **machine learning**, and **remote sensing**. My work focuses on building quantum-enhanced algorithms that can tackle real-world classification problems — like satellite crop mapping, evolutionary biology, or stellar evolution.

Currently doing **MSc in Quantum Computing** at **Université de Sherbrooke**, in collaboration with **Agriculture and Agri-Foods Canada (AAFC/AAC)**, where I develop quantum siamese networks and hybrid quantum-classical models for large-scale hyperspectral data analysis.
